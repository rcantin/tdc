Pre-Competitive Dance
This team is the perfect opportunity for those who have been dancing in recreational to experience the excitement of competitive dance. Our pre-competitive program is based on 3 disciplines (ballet, jazz, lyrical) with a minimal time commitment of 4 hours per week and attendance of up to 3 competitions. These classes are all taught by our competitive team dance staff. Ideal for the newcomer to competitive, but also full time dancers looking for a step back from the demands of a full time program. Please connect with The Dream Centre for further program details at 905-690-9022 or send us a message.

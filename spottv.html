Spot TV

You can view your child in class from the lobby, while grocery shopping, from home or work... anywhere! Our Spot TV App is available in the App store on your phone or your computer. Android users can search for “Spot TV”, and Apple users search “Go Spot TV”. Simply download the app and create a profile, and once you receive your confirmation email you’re ready to start watching classes from anywhere! Note the link for your laptop is only available on Chrome or Firefox at https://www.spottv.pro/live.

The Dreams Boutique

The Dreams Boutique is a fully stocked dancewear and gym wear store for all of your child's class attire requirements. Items include: Ballet Tights and Shoes, Bodysuits, Ballet skirts, Jazz shoes and Tights, Tap Shoes, Gym suits and shorts, leggings, Dream Centre Branded Clothing and SO MUCH MORE! We will continue to add new inventory! The Boutique is conveniently located within our facility in a separate shop within our lobby. Our staff are current dancers who are trained to fit and size your child for all their dance and gym wear needs.

Methods of payment are: Visa, Mastercard, Visa Debit or the items can be billed to your online account along with your regular tuition fees. We do not accept cash or cheque.

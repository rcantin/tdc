Our Calendar of Events is subject to change at any time and we’ll be sure to provide our families with as much notice as possible.

Tuition is based on an annual calendar and divided into 12 equal payments. Calendar months will range from 3-5 weeks of activities and events depending on holidays, other special events, weather related closures, etc.

Membership Cancelation – The Family Tree requires 30 days notice of cancelation from the first day of the next calendar month. For example, if you’re looking to cancel in March we’ll require notice on or before February 1 st .

Children must be supervised by their Parent/Guardian at all times.

Please be sure that you and your child are registered for the correct membership service based on your child’s age. If your child is currently registered in the "under 6 months" programming and is 7 months or older, your child will automatically be transferred to the older program category and your account will be billed accordingly.

Food and drinks must be kept in our lobby(Nest) at all times. Water is allowed in all Family Tree activity areas.

Additional Agreement Terms are outlined at point of registration. Please refer to your client portal that was created at point of registration to review any of these items.

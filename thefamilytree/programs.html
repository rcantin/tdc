Programs
Toddler's Open Gym
If they’re on the move then your little one will LOVE to explore our 7000 sq/ft, beautiful soft play space, open daily! Our gymnastics facility will be open daily to climb, roll, slide and create lasting memories. We will have circuits set up each day for them to explore as they socialize, learn and develop on the go. Your Littles will thrive in our high energy environment. The perfect space to release energy & enjoy new adventures every single day.
Sensory Play
Bring your Littles to our Sensory play and let them explore the world around them! It’s all about their journey and your connection as we discover a variety of sensory activities and new sensations. During this fun program, you and your Little will develop, play and discover together as you explore our sensory stations. Dive into our bins of excitement, roll & play, explore light and mirrors, play in tubs of colourful spaghetti, get messy, laugh and share an unforgettable experience.

Messy Art
A fun-filled, hands-on class where our Littles will make messes & memories. Join us and let your little one explore a variety of tactile and messy play activities they will love! It's all about creating babies artwork masterpieces through exploration. They will experiment with all different textures, strengthen their fine motor skills, gross motor skills, hand-eye coordination and use their senses to see, touch, taste, hear and feel.

Competitive Dance
Our company is a group of focused, dedicated dancers who take part in competitions, community events, and other performances throughout the year. It takes a lot of dedication and hard work on, both the part of the dancer and the parent, in order to be a part of our Award Winning Company. Dancers should have previous dance experience, excellent attendance and attitude. They must show dedication and respect to teachers, choreographers, and other students at all times as they are representatives of the studio. Our competitive team consists of Small-Frys who are ages 4-6, Minis who are ages 7-9, Juniors who are ages 10-12, Intermediates who are ages 13-15, and Seniors who are 16+. Anyone who is interested in being a part of our Competitive Company for the upcoming season will need to attend scheduled audition dates. Auditions are an opportunity for the program Director to assess student interests and ability levels, and to determine if a student is ready for the competitive stream. Our Dance Intensive is designed for the serious, dedicated dancer who is interested in being a part of one of our Award Winning Competitive or Pre-Competitive Dance Teams in the upcoming season. Dancers will participate in Ballet, Jazz, Acro, Lyrical, Contemporary, Tap, Hip Hop, and Improvisation classes, as well as, Stretch and Conditioning, and Tumbling classes in our gymnastics facility with National level coaches. This camp is mandatory for any new or returning dancer wishing to audition for one of our teams. Come and train with our Award Winning Dance and Fitness Faculty, and amazing special guest choreographers from throughout North America.

Summer Camp FAQ
Summer Camps at The Dream Centre
What to Bring
  • Lunch & Snacks (nut free please)
  • Water Bottle
  • Mask
  • Hat
  • Sunscreen
  • Running shoes
  • Change of clothes
Camp Hours
  • 9:00 am to 4:00 pm
  • No camp on holiday long weekends
Extended Care
  • 7:00 to 9:00 & 4:00 to 6:00
  • Avaiable at no extra charge!
  • Please let us know if you require Before or Aftercare. In order to provide a safe environment for your children, we need to know how many kids require extended hours so we can staff accordingly.
Pizza Lunch
  • Every Friday (for full day campers only)
  • Campers with dietary restrictions should still provide their own lunch.
Health & Safety
  • For the safety of all campers, The Dream Centre’s First Aid/CPR/AED certified staff are always a present during camp programming.
  • The Dream Centre is a nut free facility. Please do not send your children with any food containing nuts.
  • If your child requires medication of any kind to be administered to them during camp hours, please contact us prior to the start of camp.

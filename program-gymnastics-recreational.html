Gymnastics
Recreational
We strive to develop habits that promote sharing, teamwork and friendship. Developmentally appropriate gymnastics fundamentals and equipment size ensures safety and encourages children to learn at their own pace. Alongside friends and the leadership of The Dream Centresâ€™ coaches, your child will learn the foundational skills in gymnastics: Beam, bars, vault, trampoline, floor, tumbling and much more. More so, each child will experience what it feels like to succeed and be confident in playing, making friends, and mastering skills.
What to Wear
For your child's classes, please have them wear form fitted attire to any gymnastics class. When coaches are hands on and spotting children, loose fitting clothing can get caught up and be unsafe. Bodysuits and spandex shorts, tight fitting leggings, bikertards, bodysuits are all appropriate.

Birthday Parties
To book a party, call us at 905-690-9022 or send us a message.

Join us at The Dream Centre for our fantastic, all-inclusive birthday parties for kids! Choose from our selection of Dance, Gymnastics, Martial Arts themed parties and let the fun begin! Parties are two hours in length and include a pizza party with juice and snacks, and theme related activities led by our professional team of staff. Decorations, plates, utensils and napkins are also provided.

What do you need to bring? You’re welcome to show up 15 minutes before your party begins. Please be sure to bring your cake or cupcakes, loot bags, serving knife, candles and lighter! Please note that a deposit of $75 for Gymnastics and Martial Arts parties, and $125 for Princess Dance parties will automatically be applied at point of registration. Deposits are non-refundable.
